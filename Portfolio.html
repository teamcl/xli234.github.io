Industrial design

This project was my graduation project in 2012. And with this project I won 3 awards (Excellent graduation design of academy, Excellent graduation Thesis of academy and Excellent graduation Thesis of Tsinghua University). It was a intence training and during the process, how to figure out the feasible solution as soon as possible was very important. Because the bathroom is made for rural area, I could no longer use the design elements of hi-technology or sci-fi. So how to define the form style of a rural area product was also a challenge to me. To me, the most successful part was by using resource of rural area and related techology, I created a bathroom which is suitable for the country-side and local elderly and thus embody the rural area topic.
